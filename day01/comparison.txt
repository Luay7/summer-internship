Comparison, Analysis, and Conclusion

After running the code and reviewing the outputs, it is highly evident that providing clear instructions, structured details, and strict constraints leads to significantly better AI performance. Looking at the sequential progression from Prompt 1 to Prompt 5, each added element clearly increased the model's accuracy, tone, and specificity:

* Prompt 1 (Task + Word Constraint): The output was accurate but very general. This occurred because the prompt only contained a basic task without any specific format or context.
* Prompt 2 (Adding Format): The response became much clearer. By explicitly requesting an organized list with steps and requirements, the model transitioned from a standard paragraph to a highly structured answer.
* Prompt 3 (Adding Context): The output improved in relevance. By introducing the context of being a summer AI intern, the model adjusted its tone to be more encouraging and tailored the explanation perfectly to a student's learning level.
* Prompt 4 (Adding Role & Creative Tone): The output achieved a higher level of engagement. By assigning the role of an "Experienced Prompt Engineer" and requesting a creative approach, the model utilized excellent analogies (like architecting understanding) making complex technical concepts relatable.
* Prompt 5 (Strict Configuration & Constraints): This prompt yielded the most practical and high-density result. By assigning an expert role, defining a strict Key-Value structure, and setting absolute constraints (clean bullet points, no introductory filler), the model followed every rule perfectly, delivering a precise and directly actionable response.

Conclusion:
This experiment practically demonstrates that AI models are highly dependent on the quality of their inputs. The more tailored, structured, and sequentially refined a prompt is, the more accurate, controllable, and high-quality the final output will be.
